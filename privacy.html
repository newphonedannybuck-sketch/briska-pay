Briska Pay is an hours and gross-pay tracking app. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information collected

Briska Pay lets you enter work-related information such as working hours, pay rates, allowances, pay schedules, employer details, notes and app preferences. The app does not require an account. We do not sell your personal information.

Local device storage

Your work records and settings are stored locally on your device. Briska Pay does not operate a cloud synchronisation service. Anyone who can access your unlocked device may be able to view this information, so use your device security features and keep appropriate backups.

Exported files

Reports, spreadsheets and backup files are created only when you request them. You control where these files are saved or shared. Once a file is sent to another app, service or person, their privacy and security practices apply.

Google AdMob advertising

Briska Pay may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google and its partners may process device identifiers, approximate location derived from an IP address, ad interactions and diagnostic information to deliver, measure and protect advertising, subject to your consent choices and applicable law. You can learn more in Google’s Privacy Policy.

Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent

Where required, Briska Pay uses Google’s User Messaging Platform to ask for and record your advertising consent choices. You may be offered personalised or non-personalised advertising options. Where available, you can revisit your privacy choices from the app’s settings.

Analytics

Briska Pay does not use a separate analytics service to track how you use the app. AdMob may provide aggregated advertising performance and diagnostic reporting as described above.

User rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing of personal information, and to withdraw consent. Because app records are stored locally, you can review, edit, export or delete them within the app, or remove them by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. For advertising data requests, you may also need to contact Google.

Contact details

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to support@briskapay.app.
