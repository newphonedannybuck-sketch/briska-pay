Frequently asked questions

Quick answers

How do I add a work entry?

Open the calendar or entry screen, choose a date, add your start and finish times and any break, then save the entry.

Are my earnings exact?

Earnings are estimates based on the rates, hours and allowances you enter. Always compare them with your official payslip.

Where is my data stored?

Your records and settings are kept locally on your device. Briska Pay does not require an account or cloud sync.

How do I export a report?

Open Reports or Export, choose the pay period and format you need, then create the file and select where to save or share it.

Troubleshooting

Try these first

1

Check your entries

Confirm dates, start and finish times, breaks, pay rates and the selected pay period.

2

Restart and update

Close and reopen Briska Pay, then check that you are running the latest available app version.

3

Check file access

If an export fails, confirm that your device has free storage and that the chosen destination is available.

4

Keep a backup

Before clearing app data or reinstalling, create a backup and save it somewhere you can access later.

Report a bug

Found something that isn’t working?

Email us with your app version, device model, what you expected, what happened and the steps needed to reproduce it. Please do not include pay rates, employer names or other sensitive information in screenshots.
